About Us

EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute is committed to provide quality education to our students in a safe, healthy and progressive environment in order to prepare them for a better future. We ensure that the education we impart to our students makes them absolutely ready to take on the competitive industry worldwide. We encourage our students and guide them through an individualized work plan that is in line with their potential, thereby offering a wholesome and complete learning experience.

EDGE Technology & Management Institute is dedicated to our mission of shaping not only successful students, but also combining academic enrichment with the right values to frame a complete, successful and strong individual.

Mission

EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute are to:

  1. provide a learning platform for local matured working adults.
  2. provide a learning experience designed to inspire students to reach their career and personal goals.
  3. provide access to opportunities for success for all students.

Vision

  1. We prepare today’s learners for tomorrow’s communities and workplaces.
  2. We develop comprehensive programming, multiple pathways and supports for students to achieve their educational goals.
  3. We create dynamic working and learning communities that value people and support inclusion, initiative, personal development and mutual well-being.

Background & Experience

EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute Pte Ltd was set up on 24th April 2015, the Registration number is 201511067H to deliver Permitted courses, whereby Mr. Govindarajalu R Saravanan is the Director / Senior Manager of this institute. He has 15 years of experience in various industries, such as in Automobile, Mechanical, Marine, Mechatronics, Electrical and Electronics and also he has taught at various Private Diploma Education Institutes. He holds Honours Degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Coventry University, UK. He also holds Double Diploma in Mechatronics Engineering from Temasek Polytechnic, Singapore and in Automobile Engineering from CCMR Polytechnic, India. His greatest goal is to have students find learning both beneficial and enjoyable.
