EDULEDGE Attendance Policy

The student’s attendance will be monitored throughout the duration of the course.Student must meet the minimum attendance requirement, If student attendance lower than 75%, they will get:

  • Counselling with Manager
  • receive the Attendance Warning Letter
  • be barred from taking assessment

Leave of Absence policy

Absences due to Medical Grounds and Bereavement are the only two accepted valid reasons.

  • Fill in the “Application for Leave” form, Submit with supporting documents to justify the absence from class and assessment.
  • Only MCs issued by registered medical practitioners under the Singapore Medical Council will be accepted.
  • All other reasons of absence from class or assessment are subjected to case by case approval of the Manager.

    E.g. going back to home country with urgent matters during the study term.

Please submit the “Application for Leave” form with supporting documents (written request and air ticket), at least one week in advance or before you purchase your air ticket. Please provide proof of air ticket, passport (for the exit and entry immigration stamps) and supporting documents upon return.

EDULEDGE Student Transfer policy

Students who transfer from one course to another course within EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute shall be deemed to have terminated or completed the current course. The refund policy shall apply unless otherwise agreed between EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute and the student. Student is required to sign new contract for course that he/she intends to study or transfer to.

EDULEDGE Withdrawal policy

A Student who withdraws from EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute must submit the Course withdrawal Form. The refund policy shall apply unless otherwise agreed between EDULEDGE Technology & Management Institute and the student.

Refund Policy
