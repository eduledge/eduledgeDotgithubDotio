Description

The main aim of this course is to guide students on how to critically analyse and solve the problems arising from the areas of construction designing period. This course mostly concentrate on the practical grounds of technical issues such as site surveying, soil analysis, geology of the area as well the material and statistical knowledge of construction management.

This Course is developed in such a way that the individual will cast on learning new outcomes and experience how to design and handle technology, maintaining safety and construction management tools, project management and IT techniques implementation. Along with the theory and practical knowledge students will also be provided with a learning of software such as CAD design and much more for the design and construction of civil engineering projects.This course will be a simplistic guideline to students who want to pertain their future into Civil engineering as designers, planning and maintenance over construction field.

This course also demonstrates a broad knowledge of Construction Technology and Management. The curriculum recognizes and responds to the changing needs of society in the Construction industry. This course ensures that the graduate students will equip skills to sustain and excel in the dynamic Construction industry.
